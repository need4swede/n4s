About podRacer

Last updated: June 9th, 2022

podRacer Author

podRacer was written and designed by Mike Afshari

I created this application to give podcast creators and consumers a useful and intuative tool to manage their content. According to Insider Intelligence, podcast listeners account for roughly 20% of all internet users — that's 1 out of 5 people on the web today. While there are some tools out there that help users manage their podcasts, they are often first party applications with limited cross-platform compatibility and offer little help to creators with managing their metadata without having to pay for premium features. podRacer's ability to generate reports on the fly means creators can easily manage and share their content on their own platform while offering their listeners intuative ways to follow their favorite creator's work.

Another consideration are people who don't use cellular devices to consume content. With podRacer, you can load your content onto a flash drive, a CD, host it on your home media server, and more. It's a great tool for offline media consumption, which seems to be fading away in this modern age of subscription services.
